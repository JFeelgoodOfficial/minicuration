Dream Mountain

by JFeelgood

acrylic on canvas

"I want to show you the hills and valleys of my imagination."
Artist Note

Overwhelmed with thoughts and things, I closed my eyes and closed my mind feeling only the heartless darkness. I didn't need a reason to fall in love with you. I opened my heart and there you were. My paint, my brush, my canvas. This piece is purely an homage to the surrender to the wonder of imagination.

The Original Painting

The original Dream Mountain is acrylic on canvas, 24x36, painted in 2022. It currently in artist's studio and is not for sale.

Process Notes

Made of countless dabs of paint, I crafted a scene that served as a snapshot of a meadow in my mind. A place that only I could experience and share about, like anyone's memories and dreams. It's a piece that seems to paint itself with possibility.

Why This Works at Card Scale

Impasto texture and cosmic color create an otherworldly landscape that mesmerizes from any viewing distance. The swirling golden sun over vibrant purple skies and luminous green mountains generates movement and wonder, making this piece an immersive anchor for thoughtfully designed spaces.
