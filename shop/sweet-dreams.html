Sweet Dreams

by JFeelgood

acrylic on wood, joint compound

"Secret garden, sweetest dream. Hello lover, hello queen."

This began with the plants and just a place to wipe my brush when I was working on another painting. Somehow the haphazard brushstrokes began to have a look of familiarity, and I wanted to see more of these beautiful plants. It wasn't enough for me to just see them though; I wanted something like a porch or patio to sit and watch them grow. The lady figure is symbolic of art itself and somehow with this piece, I am able to experience the art I create with art anthropomorphized and myself (the artist) in one moment.

The original Sweet Dreams is acrylic on wood, joint compound, 24x36, painted in 2022. It currently in artist's studio and is not for sale.

A beautiful take away from creating this was just the tiny effort of acknowledging beauty or even the fondness for a familiar ensemble of shape, and how an immersive moment can bloom from that seed of time.

Bold graphic contrasts and emotional resonance make this work an essential minicuration centerpiece. The dramatic silhouette framed by stylized red-dotted trees creates powerful depth and contemplation, while the textured blue-gray sky and strategic color pops ensure this intimate moment commands attention in any curated space.
