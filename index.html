About Minicuration

Art that fits in the palm of your hand.

Minicuration turns original paintings into trading card-sized limited edition prints to usher art investing into the modern world while keeping it physical, collectible, and affordable. Each card is numbered, artist-credited, and magnetically sealed in an acrylic case with a display stand.

We partner with independent artists and limit every release to 50 prints. The back of each card tells the full story: artist, medium, and the concept behind the work.
